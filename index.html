CA Meena Kedia

With the objective of building financial literacy and financial discipline in the community through increased awareness and education relating to various financial jargons and financial investment needs, we are on a mission to impact lives of 1 Million people in their investment and wealth creation journey.

Meena Kedia, founder and proprietor, a thorough sincere and dedicated Chartered Accountant professional with an MNC experience of more than two decades. During her long corporate experience, she realized lack of availability of time and knowledge to put one’s hard earned money to proper use is a big challenge for all working professionals, be in male or female (moreso for females given the home / kids commitments that she has to cater to along with her ongoing work. Similar challenge is for working business households (irrespective of the sectors or type of business he / she is involved into i.e. a manufacturer, a trader, a retailer etc.).

Thanks to the competitive era that we all are living in. Home makers have a bigger challenge to put their savings into a productive usage for the money to grow given their limited environment that they spend their time and lack of adequate keenness and awareness relating to financial markets. Apart from the categories of persons mentioned above, she also found a complete lack of financial knowledge & awareness among growing children who in a couple of years would become professionals ready to kick start their life journey.

In her zeal to provide a solution to this burning issue being faced on a day-to-day basis, Meena with her undying entrepreneurial spirit and interest in investment space, founded MAK Securities to provide holistic solutions to her clients. This includes Wealth Management Services, Financial Planning Services, various investment requirement (short term, medium term, long term with full flexibility on the amount to be invested), trading needs, direct equities, mutual funds, bonds, debts, insurance, etc) of our investors / clients.

We are a Business Partner with Motilal Oswal Financial Services Limited, a well-known and well-diversified listed financial services company known for its equity research and value-based investing concepts. The firm is being recognized at various national and international forums and has been receiving various awards & recognitions. We are proud to be associated with a great firm such as Motilal Oswal in our journey of creating wealth for all our valued clients.
